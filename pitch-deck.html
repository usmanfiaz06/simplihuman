Product

Features That Resonate

😊

Mood Tracking

Highest Engagement — 33%

Our anchor feature. Users log emotions with context tags (Work, Social, Sleep, Partner, Exercise), creating rich longitudinal data. Mood Tracker 2.0 adds mini-journals and micro-reflection prompts for deeper self-awareness.

🔥

Release

Unique — No Competitor Has This

A breakthrough therapeutic mechanic. Write your thought, then choose to Burn it, Bury it, or Drown it. The thought is permanently deleted — a powerful cathartic ritual that transforms emotional processing into a tangible action.

🤖

AI Companion Chat

4 AI Personalities

Four distinct AI companions — Sage (calm & reflective), Blaze (energetic & motivating), Leo (directive coach), Grace (warm & comforting) — matched to user mood and preference for personalized support.

📢

Echo

Anonymous Community

Share thoughts anonymously. Your Echo reaches other users who can respond with support. It's the emotional equivalent of PostSecret meets Reddit's r/offmychest — creating genuine peer connection without identity exposure.

📝

Journaling

Guided & Free-form

Structured and free-form journaling with AI-powered prompts that adapt to the user's current emotional state. Premium features include photo attachments, voice journals with transcription, and exportable emotional insights.

🧘

Exercises & Mindfulness

Structured Interventions

Breathing exercises, guided meditations, and cognitive reframing tools. One-tap actions after AI conversations (e.g., "Try 1-minute breathing") turn emotional insights into immediate action.
